Behind the scenes

Stages of Airdonex Innovation



Preparation

Our Research and development department will collect and find problems that have to be solved in the field of aviation. We collect the necessary details to understand the problem at this stage.



Incubation

Once the problem is found and the necessary information about the problem is collected, we as a whole team will work on the problem. The Human resource department will help us in providing a good environment to come up with the solutions.



Illumination

During the illumination stage the innovative solutions occur. We involve all the departments to be part of this stage. We discuss our solutions and document them.



Implementation

The Operations and Logistics department and Research and development department will develop the idea into a working prototype.



Evaluation

Research and development department will evaluate and verify the prototype based on the rubrics. Once the prototype is tested and verified, it will turn into a complete innovation.



Promotion

After the long journey, our innovation will reach your hands. Our marketing department will be behind this magic.
